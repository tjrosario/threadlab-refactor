What is ThreadLab?

ThreadLab is a scientific, data-driven men's clothing service for regular guys who don't like to shop. (No subscription required.)

How does ThreadLab work?​

  1. Sign up

    Signing up for ThreadLab takes about a minute. We ask for some basic information like size, desired price ranges, and the colors you wish to avoid. This helps us and our system understand your sizes, preferences and budget.

  2. Place an order (any time)

    Placing an order is even faster than signing up. Simply select the Product Categories you need and our system will match your profile with the products we have in stock.

  3. Shipment arrives

    1. Once your shipment arrives, take up to days to make sure the clothes meet your needs.
    2. Check out by logging into Your Account and clicking on your order in Order History. Request exchanges you need to make if there were any issues with sizing, and provide feedback on anything you want so that your future orders will be even more precise.
    3. If you decide you need to return, or exchange something, no problem. We always include a self-sealing pre-paid UPS label.

  4. Give feedback that makes the service even better for you

    Provide as much feedback as possible on every item. We give you a chance to provide feedback at Order Preview and at Self Checkout. This way our system learns more about you and the clothes that will work best, making your future ordering even easier.

    Additionally, you can always give us more details about your preferences by refining your Product Preferences or Brand Preferences in Your Account.
